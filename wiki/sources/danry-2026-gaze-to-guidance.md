Danry, Hernandez, Wilson, Maes, Amores (Microsoft Research / MIT Media Lab) —
*From Gaze to Guidance: Interpreting and Adapting to Users' Cognitive Needs with
Multimodal Gaze-Aware AI Assistants*, arXiv:2604.08062, 2026 — bibliography
**[26]** (**preprint-flagged** in plan §21). Part of the
[[gaze-conditioned-ai]] cluster and a D3 cognitive-state data point.
(`raw/From Gaze to Guidance...-2026.pdf`)

## Key facts

- Multimodal **gaze-aware AI assistants** that infer users' cognitive needs
  (confusion, attention, load) from gaze and adapt guidance accordingly.
- Double relevance: (i) another proliferating reason to collect gaze
  ([[gaze-conditioned-ai]]); (ii) fresh evidence that gaze dynamics leak
  **cognitive/affective state** — vector D3 of [[leakage-vectors-d1-d6]],
  extending the tutoring lineage ([[dmello-2012-gaze-tutor]]).
