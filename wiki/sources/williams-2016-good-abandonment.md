Abandonment in Mobile Search*, **WWW '16**, doi:10.1145/2872427.2883074 **(verify

## Key facts

- Uses **reading time and touch interactions** as signals for good abandonment
  where clicks are absent — a mobile analogue of the D2 examination channel.
- For D2: supports RQ2 (zero-click intent is recoverable from interaction beyond
  clicks); D2 adds **gaze + desktop cursor** as the recovery channels.
