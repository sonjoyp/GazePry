D'Mello & Graesser (Notre Dame / Memphis) — *AutoTutor and Affective AutoTutor:
Learning by Talking with Cognitively and Emotionally Intelligent Computers that
Talk Back*, **ACM TiiS 2012** — bibliography **[2]**. Companion to
[[dmello-2012-gaze-tutor|Gaze Tutor]] in the affective-computing lineage
GazePry cites for the D3 cognitive/affective-state vector. (`raw/AutoTutor and
affective autotutor...-2012.pdf`)

## Key facts

- An intelligent tutoring system that builds a **cognitive + affective model**
  of the learner (from responses and, in the affective version, sensed
  emotional/engagement signals) and adapts its dialog.
- Cited as evidence that attention/confusion/engagement are inferable and
  actionable — the affective-state grounding for vector D3.
