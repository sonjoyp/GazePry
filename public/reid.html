    The "unclearable" point

    Conventional trackers die when the user clears state. Gaze does not: the identifier is re-derived from the live eye stream. Wipe cookies, localStorage, sessionStorage and the tracker's local model, then re-identify — the match still lands, because nothing about who you are was ever stored in the browser.
