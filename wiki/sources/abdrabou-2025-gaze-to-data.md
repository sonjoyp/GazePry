Abdrabou, Özdel, Maquiling, Bozkir, Kasneci (TU Munich) — *From Gaze to Data:
Privacy and Societal Challenges of Using Eye-tracking Data to Inform GenAI
Models*, **ETRA 2025** — bibliography **[9]**. Frames the privacy/societal
stakes of feeding **gaze into generative-AI models** — the collection-surface
expansion that also motivates the [[gaze-conditioned-ai]] papers.
(`raw/From Gaze to Data...-2025.pdf`)

## Key facts

- Argues that using eye-tracking data to train/condition **GenAI models**
  raises new privacy and societal challenges (consent, secondary use,
  inference beyond the stated purpose).
- Part of GazePry's privacy-framing / consent-angle citation set, and a bridge
  to the gaze-conditioned-LLM/VLM cluster ([[gaze-conditioned-ai]]).
