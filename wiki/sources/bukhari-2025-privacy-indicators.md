Mustafa Shah Bukhari, Sajid, Ji, David-John (Virginia Tech) — *Rethinking
Privacy Indicators in Extended Reality: Multimodal Design for Situationally
Impaired Bystanders*, **ISMAR-Adjunct 2025** — bibliography **[17]**. A
privacy-*signaling* design study (how to indicate recording to bystanders) —
background for GazePry's consent/notice discussion. (`raw/Rethinking Privacy
Indicators in Extended Reality...-2025.pdf`)

## Key facts

- Designs multimodal recording indicators (e.g. LED signals) for XR so that
  **situationally impaired bystanders** know when they are being recorded.
- Peripheral to GazePry's technical thesis, cited in the privacy-signaling /
  consent-design context — the flip side of covert capture (GazePry's threat is
  precisely *absent* such indicators for gaze extraction).
