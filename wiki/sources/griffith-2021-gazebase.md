Griffith, Lohr, Abdulin, Komogortsev (Texas State) — *GazeBase, a large-scale,
multi-stimulus, longitudinal eye movement dataset*, **Scientific Data 2021** —
bibliography **[36]**. The reference longitudinal EMB dataset; the entity page
[[gazebase]] consolidates it with its siblings. (`raw/GazeBase...-2021.pdf`)

## Key facts

- **322 college-aged participants**, **12,334 monocular recordings**, **EyeLink
  1000 @ 1000 Hz**. Seven tasks per round × two contiguous sessions: fixation,
  horizontal saccade, random oblique saccade, **reading**, two cinematic video
  free-viewing, and a gaze-driven gaming task.
- **Nine rounds over 37 months**, each round recruited only from prior rounds —
  giving the longitudinal template-aging structure GazePry's cross-session /
  [[unclearability]] claim needs.
- The dataset [[lohr-2022-eye-know-you-too|EKYT]] trains and reports its
  0.58%/3.66% EER on (TEX reading task).
