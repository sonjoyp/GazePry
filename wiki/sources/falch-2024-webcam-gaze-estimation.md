Falch & Lohan (OST Switzerland) — *Webcam-based gaze estimation for computer
screen interaction*, **Frontiers in Robotics and AI 2024**. A webcam
screen-gaze estimation study; supporting context for [[gaze-estimation]] on the
desktop surface. *Not in plan §21 — cite author-year.*
(`raw/Webcam-based gaze estimation for computer screen interaction...-2024.pdf`)

## Key facts

- Webcam-only estimation of on-screen gaze for HCI (desktop screen
  interaction), the same laptop/desktop surface GazePry treats as its primary
  novel attack surface ([[form-factor-analysis]]).
- One more open-source/commodity data point that desktop webcam gaze is
  usable for screen-region interaction.
