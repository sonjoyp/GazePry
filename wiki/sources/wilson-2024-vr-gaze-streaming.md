Wilson, Ibragimov, Proulx, Tetali, Butler, Jain — *Privacy-Preserving Gaze Data
Streaming in Immersive Interactive Virtual Reality: Robustness and User
Experience*, **IEEE TVCG 2024** — bibliography **[13]**. A robustness + UX
study of streaming gaze-privacy in *interactive* VR — completes the RQ5 defense
landscape with the privacy–utility–experience angle. (`raw/Privacy-Preserving
Gaze Data Streaming...-2024.pdf`)

## Key facts

- Extends the streaming gaze-privacy line ([[david-john-2021-streaming-privacy]])
  to **immersive, interactive** VR and measures both **robustness** of the
  protection and its effect on **user experience** — the "utility cost" side of
  GazePry's privacy–utility curve.
- Reinforces the plan's framing that a defense is only publishable paired with a
  measured utility/experience cost, not privacy in isolation.
