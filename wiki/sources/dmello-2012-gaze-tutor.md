D'Mello, Olney, Williams, Hays (Notre Dame / Memphis) — *Gaze tutor: A
gaze-reactive intelligent tutoring system*, **Int. J. Human-Computer Studies
2012** — bibliography **[1]**. Foundational evidence that gaze reveals
**cognitive/affective state** (boredom, disengagement) — the D3 lineage.
(`raw/Gaze tutor...-2012.pdf`. A ScienceDirect HTML mirror, `S1071581912000250.html`,
was in `raw/` but deleted in commit 9185c26.)

## Key facts

- A tutoring system that **reacts to the learner's gaze**: detects boredom /
  disengagement from gaze patterns and intervenes to redirect attention.
- Early, well-cited evidence that content-*independent* gaze dynamics carry
  **attention/engagement/affect** — vector D3 of [[leakage-vectors-d1-d6]],
  now modernized by [[danry-2026-gaze-to-guidance]] and webcam-grade detection
  ([[hutt-2024-mind-wandering]]).
